Who We Are

Guarding Every Layer of the Nation's Cyberspace

The National Cyber Emergency Response Team (NCERT) is a dedicated body responsible for safeguarding national digital infrastructure. We coordinate the detection, prevention, and response to cyber security incidents across government, critical infrastructure, and private sector networks.

Our multidisciplinary teams combine security operations, forensic investigation, secure software engineering, and governance expertise to build a resilient and trustworthy digital ecosystem for every citizen.

  • Rapid incident detection and coordinated response
  • National-scale threat intelligence sharing
  • Digital forensics and compliance advisory

Our Process

How NCERT Functions

Every incident is handled through a structured, repeatable response lifecycle from first detection to final review.

01

Threat Detection

Continuous monitoring of networks, systems, and endpoints to identify suspicious activity and potential threats in real time.

02

Alert Generation

Automated detection systems and SOC analysts raise alerts the moment anomalous or malicious behavior is observed.

03

Incident Analysis

Security analysts investigate each alert to determine the scope, severity, and root nature of the incident.

04

Containment

Immediate action isolates affected systems to stop the threat from spreading further across the network.

05

Eradication

The root cause of the incident is identified and completely removed from the affected environment.

06

Recovery

Affected systems and services are restored to normal operation, with verification that no residual threats remain.

07

Reporting

A detailed incident report documents the timeline, impact, and every action taken during the response.

08

Lessons Learned

Each incident is reviewed to strengthen future defenses by improving policies, tools, and processes.
